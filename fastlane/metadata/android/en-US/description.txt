Miko is a high-performance, ultra-minimalist manga reader built with Kotlin and Jetpack Compose. It follows the Material 3 Expressive design guidelines to provide a fluid, modern, and highly customizable reading experience.

Features:
- Performance-First: Fast image streaming using Coil.
- Material 3 Expressive: Large corner radii, bold typography, and seamless transitions.
- Deep Customization: Dynamic Color, Theme Modes (Light/Dark), and color palettes (Lavender, Forest, etc.).
- Powered by MangaDex: Access a massive library of manga with built-in search.
- Flexible Reader: Supports both Vertical (Webtoon) and Paged reading modes.
- History & Library: Automatically tracks reading progress and saves favorites locally.
